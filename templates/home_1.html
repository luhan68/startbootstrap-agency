The Department of Statistics & Applied Probability and the Center for Financial Mathematics and Actuarial Research are partnering with Hull Tactical to run a data science contest on predicting the S&P 500 returns. Undergraduate and Masters students may participate in teams of up to 4 members, to compete for total prizes of $7000 in two categories: (i) Best Prediction and (ii) Most Creative. The prizes up for grab include $2500 for the winner and $1000 for the runner-up in each catergory.

Timeline

Registration

Register your team here or search for like-minded teammates here. Here is the list of everyone who searched for a team. Registration ends on March 15th. You are allowed to register a team of up to 4 students (currently enrolled at UCSB). At most one Master's student can participate per team and no PhD students are allowed entry.
